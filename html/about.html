I'm Kenia Mancillas-Herrera, a junior web developer with a passion for leveraging technology to make a positive impact. My journey into web development began with a need to provide for my family and has evolved into a deep-seated desire to use my skills for the betterment of my community and, eventually, the world.

My primary goal is to utilize my newfound skills to contribute meaningfully to my local community. I am driven by a profound sense of responsibility to use my skills for the greater good.
